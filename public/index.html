Our History

Studio Hair

Ana Rios is the owner of Studio Hair, a Hair Salon Spa in Denver, Colorado.

She has a background in the beauty field, and ten years of experience as a Hairdresser.

Ana opened Hair Studio to blend her passion for beauty and aesthetics. She aims to help clients enhance their natural beauty and feel confident.
